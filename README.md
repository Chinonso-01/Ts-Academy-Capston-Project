# 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales Performance Dashboard** was developed to monitor and evaluate:

* Revenue generation
* Cost of goods sold (COGS)
* Profit performance
* Sales quantity
* Product profitability
* Sales representative contributions

The dashboard enables users to filter and explore data dynamically across:

* Regions (East, North, South, West)
* Product categories (Electronics, Furniture, Home Appliances)

---

# 🚀 Dashboard Features

✔ Interactive filters for region and category
✔ KPI summary cards for quick insights
✔ Trend analysis (daily and monthly)
✔ Product-level profit comparison
✔ State-level cost distribution
✔ Sales rep performance tracking

# 📈 Visualizations Explained

### 1. Daily Trend

Shows day-to-day fluctuations in sales performance

### 2. Monthly Trend

Displays sales progression across months

### 3. Product Profit

Highlights top-performing and low-performing products

### 4. State COGS (Pie Chart)

Shows cost distribution across states

### 5. Sales Rep by Revenue

Compares performance of sales representatives

---

# 💡 Insights Derived

* High-performing products contribute most to profit
* Revenue distribution varies across sales reps
* Some regions incur higher operational costs
* Sales trends show possible seasonality
* A few sales reps drive most of the revenue
